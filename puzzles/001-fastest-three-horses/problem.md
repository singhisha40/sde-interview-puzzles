# 🐎 Problem: Find the 3 Fastest Horses

**Source:** GeeksForGeeks 100 Puzzles  
**Puzzle #:** 001  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Topic:** Logic / Elimination

---

## Problem Statement

You have **25 horses** and a racing track where you can race at most **5 horses at a time**.

You have **no stopwatch** — after each race you only know the finishing order of those 5 horses, not their actual times.

**Find the minimum number of races required to determine the top 3 fastest horses.**

---

## Constraints

- Exactly 5 horses per race, no more, no less
- No timing device — only relative order within a race is known
- A horse always runs at the same speed (consistent performance)
- You must be 100% certain of your top 3 — no guessing

---

## Expected Answer

**7 races**
